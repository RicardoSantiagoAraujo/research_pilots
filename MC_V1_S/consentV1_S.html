Consent

Welcome to our web-based experiment. Before taking part in this study, please read the consent form below and click on the I Agree button at the bottom of the page if you understand the statements and freely consent to participate in the study. You are eligible to participate if you are over the age of 18, however, your participation is entirely voluntary.

Who is conducting this study?

This study is part of a collaborative project based in Toulouse, France, and brings together researchers from EDB (Laboratoire Évolution & Diversité Biologique), IAST (Institute For Advanced Study in Toulouse) and CRCA (Centre de Recherches sur la Cognition Animale).

What does the experiment consist of?

You will first be asked to complete a short questionnaire about yourself. We will then ask you to complete two types of tasks. In one, you are asked to rate male or female faces based on their attractiveness. In the other, you are asked to answer simple questions about faces, such as identifying hair color or estimating age. The study will take between 5 and 10 minutes to complete. Further details on the purpose of the study will be provided at the end of the experiment.

Will my information be kept confidential?

You will remain anonymous and you have the right to stop at any time during the study, with no negative consequences, by closing this web page. No identifying information will be asked about you. As the information you provide in this study is anonymous, it will not be possible to withdraw your data upon completion of the study.

Are there any risks associated with taking part in this study?

No harm will come to you from taking part in this experiment.
